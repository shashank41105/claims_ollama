BUPA MEDICAL INSURANCE POLICY
================================

POLICY HANDBOOK - 2024
======================

SECTION 1: COVERAGE OVERVIEW
-----------------------------

This policy provides comprehensive medical coverage for policyholders and their dependents. The policy covers a wide range of medical services, treatments, and procedures.

SECTION 2: COVERED SERVICES
---------------------------

The following services are covered under this policy:

1. General Practitioner Consultations
   - Maximum coverage: ₹5,000 per visit
   - Annual limit: ₹50,000
   - No prior authorization required

2. Specialist Consultations
   - Maximum coverage: ₹10,000 per visit
   - Annual limit: ₹100,000
   - Prior authorization required for certain specialties

3. Prescribed Western Medication
   - Coverage: 80% of prescription cost
   - Maximum per prescription: ₹15,000
   - Annual limit: ₹150,000

4. Diagnostic Imaging & Laboratory Tests
   - X-rays, CT scans, MRI: 90% coverage
   - Blood tests, urinalysis: 100% coverage
   - Annual limit: ₹200,000

5. Health Check-ups
   - Annual preventive check-up: 100% coverage
   - Maximum: ₹8,000 per year

6. Alternative Medicine
   - Bonesetter: ₹3,000 per session
   - Physiotherapist: ₹5,000 per session
   - Chiropractor: ₹5,000 per session
   - Annual limit combined: ₹50,000

7. Vaccination
   - Routine vaccinations: 100% coverage
   - Travel vaccinations: 80% coverage
   - Annual limit: ₹10,000

8. Dental Care
   - Routine check-ups: 100% coverage
   - Dental procedures: 70% coverage
   - Annual limit: ₹30,000

9. Mental Health Services
   - Psychiatric consultations: ₹8,000 per session
   - Counseling: ₹5,000 per session
   - Annual limit: ₹80,000

SECTION 3: CLAIM SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
----------------------------------------

To submit a claim, policyholders must provide:

1. Completed claim form
2. Original itemized medical bills and receipts
3. Medical reports and prescriptions
4. Diagnostic test results (if applicable)
5. Proof of payment
6. Valid policy certificate

Required Information on Medical Bills:
- Patient's full name (must match policy)
- Date of service
- Detailed diagnosis
- Itemized breakdown of charges
- Provider's name and credentials
- Receipt number and date

SECTION 4: GENERAL EXCLUSIONS
-----------------------------

The following conditions and treatments are NOT covered under this policy:

1. Pre-existing Conditions
   - Any condition that existed before policy inception
   - Waiting period: 24 months

2. HIV/AIDS Related Treatment
   - All treatments, medications, and consultations
   - Includes AIDS-related complex conditions

3. Sexually Transmitted Diseases (STDs)
   - Treatment for all STDs
   - Except when contracted through blood transfusion

4. Pregnancy and Maternity
   - Prenatal care, delivery, and postnatal care
   - Complications of pregnancy
   - Separate maternity rider available

5. Cosmetic Procedures
   - Plastic surgery for aesthetic purposes
   - Dental cosmetics
   - Exception: Reconstructive surgery after accidents

6. Self-Inflicted Injuries
   - Injuries from suicide attempts
   - Self-harm
   - Injuries sustained while committing a crime

7. Substance Abuse
   - Alcohol abuse treatment
   - Drug addiction rehabilitation
   - Related complications

8. Experimental Treatments
   - Treatments not approved by medical authorities
   - Clinical trials
   - Unproven therapies

9. Chronic Degenerative Conditions
   - Alzheimer's disease
   - Parkinson's disease
   - Advanced stage conditions with no curative treatment

10. War and Nuclear Hazards
    - Injuries from war, terrorism, riots
    - Nuclear radiation exposure

SECTION 5: CLAIM PROCESSING
---------------------------

Standard Claims:
- Processing time: 7-10 business days
- Notification via email and SMS
- Payment via direct bank transfer

Emergency Claims:
- Fast-track processing: 48-72 hours
- Requires emergency room documentation
- Prior authorization waived

Required Documentation:
1. Valid photo ID
2. Policy certificate
3. Original medical bills
4. Medical reports signed by licensed physician
5. Prescription with doctor's signature and registration number

SECTION 6: FRAUD PREVENTION
---------------------------

The insurer reserves the right to:
- Investigate suspicious claims
- Request additional documentation
- Conduct medical examinations
- Verify treatment with healthcare providers
- Reject claims found to be fraudulent

Warning Signs of Fraud:
- Inflated bills or duplicate charges
- Services not rendered
- Altered documents
- Misrepresented diagnosis
- Provider kickbacks

SECTION 7: POLICY LIMITS
------------------------

Annual Maximum Benefit: ₹1,000,000
Lifetime Maximum Benefit: ₹5,000,000

Per-incident Limits:
- Hospitalization: ₹500,000
- ICU care: ₹300,000
- Surgery: ₹400,000
- Ambulance: ₹5,000

SECTION 8: CO-PAYMENT
---------------------

Standard co-payment: 10% of covered expenses
Senior citizens (60+): 15% co-payment
Children under 18: 5% co-payment

Maximum out-of-pocket: ₹100,000 per year

SECTION 9: NETWORK PROVIDERS
----------------------------

Preferred provider discounts:
- Apollo Hospitals: 20% discount
- Max Healthcare: 15% discount
- Fortis Healthcare: 15% discount
- Manipal Hospitals: 10% discount

Out-of-network: Standard rates apply

SECTION 10: APPEALS PROCESS
---------------------------

If a claim is denied:
1. Review denial letter for specific reasons
2. Submit appeal within 30 days
3. Include additional documentation
4. Appeals review: 15-20 business days
5. Final decision communicated in writing
